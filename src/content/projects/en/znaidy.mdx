## Task

I ran into the problem of job searching as a product designer. вакансії were scattered across dozens of platforms — Dribbble, Behance, Remotive, and others. I was spending hours checking everything manually. So I decided to build my own platform that aggregates design jobs in one place.

## Solution

I built the full product cycle myself — from design to development. I created a complex data collection system with n8n workflows that automatically parse vacancies from Dribbble, Behance, and Remotive and store them in Supabase.

I developed the frontend in Next.js with a filtering system by category, location, and employment type. I added a unique Salary Index widget that shows the minimum, median, and maximum salary plus the market trend.

I also created a full admin panel for vacancy management with built-in analytics. I designed the entire experience using my product design background, and implemented development through a combination of coding skills and no-code tools.

## Result

The platform is live in MVP stage and is gradually filling with design vacancies. I solved my own problem and created a tool that saves time for other designers. The next step is expanding into other specialties and adding more job sources.
