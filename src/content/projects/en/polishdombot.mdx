## Task

I was approached by a Polish language tutor. She said: "I prepare students for the Karta Polaka, but I don't have enough time to run mock interviews with everyone." She needed a tool where students could practice on their own and receive an objective readiness assessment, similar to what they would get from a real consul.

## Solution

I created a Telegram bot with two preparation modes. The first is a classic language level test for a basic knowledge assessment. The second is a full simulation of the Karta Polaka interview.

The hardest part was the consular interview simulation mode. The bot asks the same kinds of questions that appear in real interviews, including Polish history, culture, and personal reasons for obtaining the card. Students can answer either by voice or by text.

AI then analyzes each response across several criteria: language accuracy, completeness, factual knowledge, and naturalness of explanation. After that, the bot generates detailed feedback in the voice of a consul, pointing out weak spots, what needs improvement, and most importantly whether the student would likely pass the real interview at that stage.

## Result

The tutor received an automated preparation tool. Students can now practice an unlimited number of times without the stress of a real interview. They get concrete feedback on what to improve, while the tutor can focus on difficult cases instead of routine checks.
