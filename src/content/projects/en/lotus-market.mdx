## Task

A client approached me with an idea to create an analytical service for DeFi tokens. He said that 90% of tokens in the crypto space are scams, and investors are losing money. He needed a spacious dashboard page to show investors the project's potential and attract funding.

The main problem was how to visualize complex blockchain analytics in a way that investors could understand.

## Solution

I created a dashboard that shows key metrics for tokens on Ethereum and BSC: liquidity, holder distribution, rating score, and smart contract analysis. I designed an interface where investors can evaluate the quality of a project in 30 seconds.

Together with the client, we went from concept to basic MVP. I created a filter system that weeds out scam tokens from quality projects. I worked on data visualization—graphs, indicators, scoring. Created a design for two pricing plans — basic and premium analytics. Took into account the difference in token behavior on BSC and Ethereum in the interface.

## Result

Lotus Market launched in beta and is still running with my basic design. The platform attracted users with two subscription plans ($40 and $90/month). After completing the MVP phase, I moved on to other projects, and the team continued development independently with minor design changes.
