## Task

I was approached by a brokerage company that managed its client base using scattered Excel spreadsheets and messengers. Managers spent 2-3 hours a day just searching for information about clients. There was no common understanding of who was working with whom, what deposits the client had, or when they last spoke with them.

## Solution

I designed a full-fledged CRM from scratch. I created a dashboard with 15 widgets for instant understanding of the situation — from daily income to the latest leads. I created a customer base with 25 parameters and smart filters. Added a role system for 7 types of employees — from managers to affiliates. Worked out integration with trading platforms and telephony.

Built a design system with 100+ components that covers all usage scenarios. Took into account that the system would grow — laid out a modular interface architecture.

## Result

As a result, managers now spend 15 minutes searching for information instead of 2-3 hours. Lead processing speed increased 4 times. The company was able to increase its customer base 3 times without hiring additional managers.
